XenoStasis


Xenostasis, an online co-op horror game up to Four players using either PC or a VR Headset, You work as an XRT agent with the goal of entering locations in outer space and repairing them, all while dangerous Xenos are our to kill you and your crew,


Four Player Crews


If you are using a PC or want to immerse yourself with a VR Headset, Crew's will require teamwork and good communication if you want to make it out alive....

Intense Scenarios and Equipment


This game isnt always about fast paced action. Missions will start with special equipment that must be fine tuned to the specific scenario. Each mission will have randomly generated scenarios. You must work together in order to survive......

Desolate Locations


The Game Expands across many different locations in Early Access with many more on the way. Each Location offers different gameplay elements and places such as Space Stations, Ships, Underground facilities and even Alien Planets...

Deadly Aliens


Hence the name, the game offers many different types of Xenos that will require you and your crew to plan out a way to get rid of them. Xenos have unique abilities as well as weaknesses. Do your research or end up dead....
